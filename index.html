Company Overview

Established in 2018, Maitekomantle Holdings (Pty) Ltd is a 100% black-owned and youth-managed company, proudly contributing at Level 1 B-BBEE status. Our core focus lies in Civil Engineering Works, Logistics, and Transportation Services, with expansion into house maintenance, supply, and delivery solutions tailored to meet our clients' organizational requirements.

Outstanding service and quality are at the forefront of our operations. We leverage our strategic relationships with stakeholders and strong technical expertise to deliver results that exceed client expectations.
